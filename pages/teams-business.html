About the Team

Our business team powers EWB Cornell through strategic fundraising, community engagement, and operational excellence. We've raised $60,000+ through crowdfunding campaigns and secured partnerships with Cornell departments and local businesses. We also develop educational programs to spread engineering awareness and manage the organization's brand and communications.

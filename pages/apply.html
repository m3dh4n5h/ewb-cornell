FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior engineering experience?

Not at all. We welcome students from every major and experience level. Enthusiasm, commitment, and a desire to learn matter most. Our teams actively mentor new members.

How much time commitment is required?

Expect 3–6 hours per week for team meetings, project work, and events. Some teams have additional commitments during intensive design phases or site visit prep.

Can I join multiple teams?

We recommend starting with one team to fully commit and get the most out of your experience. After establishing yourself, cross-team contributions may be possible.

When are applications reviewed?

We review applications on a rolling basis. Most new member intake happens at the start of each semester, but we occasionally accept members mid-semester when spots open.

Is there a GPA requirement?

There is no minimum GPA requirement. We care about your passion for humanitarian work and your ability to commit to a team — not your transcript.

What happens after I submit?

You'll hear back within 1–2 weeks. Selected applicants are invited for a brief informal conversation with team leads. The goal is mutual fit, not a formal interview.

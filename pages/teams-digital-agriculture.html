Team Overview

About the Team

Our digital agriculture team develops non-commercial, open-source UAV/UGV systems with deep learning to detect crop diseases early, protecting food security in Sub-Saharan Africa. We focus on Northern Leaf Blight detection in maize crops, working to provide farmers with affordable early-warning systems that prevent devastating crop losses.
